About the event

22 - 23 September 2022

IET Renewable Power Generation (RPG) brings together the topics of renewable energy technology, power generation and systems integration, with techno-economic issues. All renewable energy generation technologies are within the scope of the conference. RPG provides an opportunity to connect with international academics, researchers, engineers and consultants working in renewable energy, to publish results, share knowledge and forge new partnerships.

Benefits of attending

  • Renewable power is rapidly advancing to meet climate de-carbonisation targets.
  • RPG brings innovators from industry and academia together to share their ideas and plans.
  • Whether you present a case study or research paper you will be noticed, and learn from others.
  • If you attend to hear the latest innovations you will take valuable knowledge away and have the chance to meet future collaborators in your field.
  • An invited speaker session will show-case important industry case studies.
